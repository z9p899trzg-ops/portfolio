Approach

THE SCHOOL

Photography carries the emotion. Type carries the information. Texture makes it feel printed. And the small stuff — dates, coordinates, attendance numbers — makes it feel real.

01 / REAL DATA ONLY

No lorem ipsum, no fake stats. A poster with the actual attendance number feels like an artifact. Filler feels like a template.

02 / SYSTEMS OVER ONE-OFFS

One good post is luck. Twenty-eight templates and a calendar is a machine the client can run without me — that's what I sell.

03 / GRAIN ALWAYS

Digital-clean is forbidden. Everything gets texture, contrast, one temperature push — like it came off a press.

04 / IF IT NEEDS A CAPTION, IT FAILED

The design explains itself or it dies on the table. Three colors max, one alignment spine, no drop shadows. Ever.

05 / CHANGE 3%, NOT EVERYTHING

Ramen Rush kept its logo, colors and mascot — I turned the execution up. The best redesign is the one the regulars don't notice but the numbers do.
